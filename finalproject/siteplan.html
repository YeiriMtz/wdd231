Site Name

Name: Zu’s Creative Bliss

Description: This name reflects the brand of the artist and emphasizes the personal, artistic, and professional approach to DIY art that she offers. It’s clear, memorable, and directly communicates the service provided.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a digital portfolio for Zulie’s art aka Zu’s Creative Bliss”. It includes a biography with a portrait, a sample gallery off artwork with price tags, and an easy way for potential clients to get in touch or book a custom art session.

Visitor Scenarios

  • How can I buy some of Zulie's artwork?
  • Where can I see examples of Zulie's past artwork?
  • How do I contact Zulie with questions or custom session requests?

Color Schema

  • Background 1: #9f2e76 — Used for the page background.
  • Background 2: #5c1b44 - Used for the header and footer backgrounds.
  • Primary Color: #ffc0cb — Used for buttons, hover states, and call-to-action elements.
  • Accent 1: #000000 — Used for main text.
  • Accent 2:#ffffff — Used for header and footer text and some contrast

Typography

  • Shadows Into Light (Google Font) — Used for main headings (e.g., site title).
  • Sans-serif — Used for body text, form inputs, and descriptions for clean readability.
