My creative journey began with teaching expressive art therapy, where I discovered the profound impact that art can have on personal healing and emotional expression.

Over time, I was drawn to abstract art and resin work as a way to process my own emotions and find clarity. What started as a personal practice soon became a passion to share the transformative power of creativity with others.

My art is a reflection of my journey, one of self-exploration, growth, and the stillness that comes through creative expression. I hope my work resonates with you, offering both beauty and peace.
